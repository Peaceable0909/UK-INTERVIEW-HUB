Module 1 — Preparation

Full Preparation Guide

What is a Credibility Interview?

BPP University conducts credibility interviews as part of the UK student visa sponsorship process. The interview assesses whether you are a genuine student intending to study in the UK.

verified

Verify Intent

Confirm you genuinely intend to study

account_balance

Check Finances

Ensure you understand funding requirements

work

Career Alignment

Assess how the course fits your goals

Interview Format

1

Pre-Interview Email

You'll receive a Microsoft Teams link 24-48 hours before your scheduled slot

2

Identity Check

Show your passport to the camera at the start of the call

3

Question Session (15-25 mins)

Answer questions about your course, finances, and career plans

4

Decision Timeline

Results typically communicated within 5 working days

How to Prepare

  • task_alt

    Research BPP thoroughly

    Know your campus location, module structure, and what makes BPP unique (employer-focused teaching, industry links, flexible learning)

  • task_alt

    Prepare your "Why BPP?" answer

    Connect the course content to your career goals. Mention specific modules, teaching methods, or BPP's industry partnerships

  • task_alt

    Review your CAS statement

    Know your course start date, tuition fees, and any conditions listed

  • task_alt

    Practice aloud

    Record yourself answering common questions. Focus on clear, concise responses (60-90 seconds each)

school About BPP University

  • • Part of BPP Holdings, a leading professional education provider

account_balance Visa Requirements

  • • Valid CAS (Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies)
  • • Proof of funds: £1,334/month for London, £1,023/month outside London
  • • English language proficiency (IELTS/SELT if required)
  • • TB test certificate (if applicable to your country)
  • • Valid passport with at least 6 months validity

Module 3 — Question Bank

All Interview Questions

Organised by category. Click to expand and view sample prompts.

P1 Tell me about yourself and your background.
expand_more

Focus on: education, work experience, motivation for further study. Keep it concise (60-90 seconds).

P2 Why do you want to study in the UK?
expand_more

Mention: UK's reputation for education, English language immersion, post-study work opportunities (Graduate Route visa).

C1 Why did you choose this specific programme?
expand_more

Reference specific modules, skills you'll gain, and how they align with your career goals. Avoid generic answers.

C2 What modules are you most excited about and why?
expand_more

Name 2-3 modules from the course specification. Explain their relevance to your interests or career path.

B1 Why did you choose BPP University over other institutions?
expand_more

Highlight: employer-focused curriculum, industry-experienced tutors, flexible study options, strong graduate outcomes, London/campus location benefits.

B2 What do you know about BPP's teaching approach?
expand_more

Mention: practical, career-focused learning; real-world case studies; industry guest speakers; small class sizes; blended learning options.

F1 How will you fund your tuition fees and living costs?
expand_more

Be specific: personal savings, family support, scholarship name/amount, part-time work plans (within visa limits). Show you've budgeted realistically.

U1 What are your plans after completing your studies?
expand_more

Mention: using the Graduate Route visa to gain UK work experience, returning home to apply skills, or further study. Show clear, realistic planning.

Module 4 — Examples

Sample Answers

High-quality responses to common questions. Use as inspiration—personalise your own answers.

B1

Why did you choose BPP University?

"I chose BPP University because of its strong reputation for career-focused education and direct links with industry. The [Programme Name] curriculum includes practical modules like [Specific Module], which align perfectly with my goal to work in [Career Field]."

"I was particularly impressed by BPP's teaching approach—learning from tutors with real-world experience and the opportunity for work-integrated learning. The flexible study options also allow me to balance my studies with gaining practical experience."

"Additionally, BPP's campuses in central London provide excellent networking opportunities and access to internships with leading companies, which is crucial for my professional development."

check_circle ✅ Specific, personalised, shows research
F1 How will you fund your studies?

"I have secured funding through a combination of personal savings and family support. My family has set aside £[Amount] specifically for my tuition fees, which covers the full cost of the programme."

"For living expenses, I have budgeted £[Amount] per month based on BPP's guidance for London students. I also plan to work part-time within the 20-hour weekly limit allowed on my student visa to supplement my income and gain UK work experience."

"I have researched accommodation options near the [Campus Name] campus and identified several student residences within my budget. I am confident my financial plan is realistic and sustainable for the duration of my studies."

check_circle ✅ Specific figures, shows planning, mentions visa rules
U1 What are your post-study plans?

"After completing my degree, I plan to utilise the UK's Graduate Route visa to gain professional work experience in [Industry] for up to two years. This will allow me to apply the practical skills I've learned at BPP in a real-world UK business environment."

"Long-term, I aim to return to [Home Country] to contribute to the growing [Sector] industry there. The UK qualification and work experience will give me a competitive advantage and enable me to bring international best practices back home."

"I've already researched companies in both the UK and [Home Country] that align with my career goals, and I'm confident this programme will provide the foundation I need to succeed."

check_circle ✅ Realistic, shows understanding of visa options, clear career path
